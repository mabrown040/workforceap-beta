# Interview Question Bank

Common behavioral and technical questions to prepare for.

## Behavioral (STAR Method)

- Tell me about a time you overcame a challenge.
- Describe a situation where you worked with a difficult team member.
- Give an example of when you had to meet a tight deadline.
- Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned.
- Describe a project you're proud of.

## Technical Basics (General)

- Walk me through your experience with [relevant tool/skill].
- How do you stay current in your field?
- Describe your approach to problem-solving.
- What's your experience with [software/platform]?

## Questions to Ask Them

- What does success look like in this role in the first 90 days?
- What do you enjoy most about working here?
- What are the biggest challenges the team is facing?
- How does the team collaborate?
