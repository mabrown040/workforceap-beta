What We Do

Breaking systemic barriers through education, technology, and opportunity

Our Mission

Workforce Advancement Project bridges the gap between people, talent, skills, and opportunity. Through partnerships with US.Gov, DOL, State of Texas, TWC, Workforce Solutions Capital Area, Coursera, AT&T, Google, Microsoft, and IBM we provide industry-recognized training in high-demand sectors.

We serve underserved individuals, adult learners, and veteran populations — providing free, funded pathways to careers that pay $50,000–$120,000+.

Our Approach

WorkforceAP was founded by Michael Brown, PMP — Executive Director, CEO — a veteran workforce leader who has trained thousands of Austinites through Consulting Solutions.Net, Goodwill Career & Technical Education, and the Austin Area Urban League.
