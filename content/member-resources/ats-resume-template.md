# ATS-Friendly Resume Template

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan resumes for keywords and structure. Use this template to maximize your chances of passing the filter.

## Key Principles

- **Use standard section headers**: Experience, Education, Skills (avoid graphics or tables)
- **Include keywords from the job description** in your skills and experience bullets
- **Save as .docx or PDF** — avoid complex formatting
- **Use a simple, clean font** (Arial, Calibri, 11–12pt)

## Template Structure

1. **Contact** — Name, phone, email, LinkedIn (one line each)
2. **Summary** — 2–3 sentences with target role and key strengths
3. **Experience** — Company, title, dates; bullets with action verbs and metrics
4. **Education** — Degree, school, year
5. **Skills** — Keywords grouped by category (Technical, Soft Skills)

## Download

Use your word processor to create a new document following this structure. Avoid images, headers/footers, and multi-column layouts.
