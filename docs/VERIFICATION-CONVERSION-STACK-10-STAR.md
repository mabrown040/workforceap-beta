# Verification: Conversion Stack 10-Star Upgrade

## Overview

Unified 10-star upgrade for the trust-and-conversion stack: `/apply`, `/how-it-works`, and `/faq`. Treats these as one connected system. Key strategic framing: Austin is the launch wedge, not the long-term ceiling — copy is future-proofed for national expansion.

## Changes by Page

### `/apply`

**Apply page:**
- Metadata: Title/description no longer Austin-only; "Currently serving the Austin area with plans to expand"
- Hero: Welcoming, high-conviction ("Apply for no-cost training — we'll help you find the right path")
- **Eligibility note:** "Where we operate today: We're currently serving the Austin area. This is our launch community — we're building toward expansion. If you're elsewhere, apply anyway; we'll keep you in the loop."
- Alert: Shortened to "Seats are limited. Apply today to hold your spot."

**ApplyEligibilityClient (Step 1):**
- Title: "Quick check — we want to help you find the right fit" (was "Let's see what you qualify for")
- Desc: "These answers help us point you to the right program and funding options. Everyone who applies gets a personal response."

**Apply results (Step 2):**
- Qualifiers: "Looks like a good fit. We'll connect within 24–48 hours to walk through next steps..."
- Non-qualifiers: New "apply anyway" block — "We'd still love to connect. Choose a program below — we'll review your application and help identify the best path for you, including other resources if our current funding doesn't fit."

**Create account (Step 3):**
- Subtitle: "Create your account — we'll reach out within 24–48 hours to confirm your spot and next steps."

### `/how-it-works`

- Metadata: "Your path from application through certification and job placement. Eleven clear steps — each designed to set you up for success."
- Subtitle: Confidence-building ("each designed to set you up for success")
- PhotoHighlight: "We explain what each step does for you and why it exists — so you know what to expect and can move forward with confidence."

**Per-step "Why we do this" explanations:**
- Apply: "So we can personalize your path instead of sending you into a generic funnel."
- Overview: "You deserve to know exactly what you're signing up for."
- Interview: "Mutual fit matters. We succeed when you succeed."
- Membership: "We remove money as a barrier so you can focus on learning."
- Assessment: "The right program for you is the one that fits your situation and goals."
- Workforce Readiness: "Credentials open doors; readiness gets you through them."
- Resources: "You shouldn't have to figure it all out alone."
- Training: "Real credentials, not certificates of attendance."
- Certify: "Your resume needs more than 'I took a class.'"
- Job Placement: "We're invested in your first hire, not just your last exam."
- Better Life: "This is the outcome we're both working toward."

Step descriptions rewritten to emphasize what it does for the applicant and why the process exists.

### `/faq`

- Metadata: "For applicants, parents, partners, and anyone with questions."
- Subtitle: "Answers that address your concerns — whether you're applying, supporting someone who is, or deciding if WorkforceAP is right for you."
- PhotoHighlight: "Honest answers about how we work, what we expect, and what you can expect from us."

**New category: Common Concerns**
- Is this too good to be true?
- How do you make money if training is free?
- I've been burned by programs before. Why should I trust this?
- What if I'm not tech-savvy?

**New category: For Families & Partners**
- My family member is considering this. What should I know?
- As a partner, how do I know this is legitimate?
- Can I refer someone who lives outside Austin?

**Updated existing answers:**
- Admissions: Austin as "currently we serve... expanding over time"
- Application process: "No test, no gatekeeping — we use the application to help you, not to screen you out"
- Programs online: "Currently concentrated in the Austin area; format may vary as we expand"
- Qualifying: "Currently serving the Austin area; we're expanding"

## Austin-as-Launch-Wedge Framing

- Austin referenced as "current" or "launch" operations, not permanent ceiling
- "We're expanding" or "building toward expansion" where relevant
- Non-Austin visitors encouraged to apply; "we'll keep you in the loop"

## Preserved

- Dad's voice and brand tone
- No generic nonprofit slop
- Credibility (leadership, experience, no loans/ISAs)
- Phone number and contact options

## Verification Steps

1. **Apply:** Visit /apply — confirm eligibility note, welcoming hero, softened eligibility questions.
2. **Apply flow:** Complete as qualifier and non-qualifier — confirm both paths feel guided, not cold-screened.
3. **How it works:** Visit /how-it-works — confirm "Why we do this" on each step, confidence-building copy.
4. **FAQ:** Visit /faq — confirm Common Concerns and For Families & Partners tabs, Austin-as-launch language in relevant answers.
5. **Metadata:** Check page titles/descriptions — no permanent Austin-only locking.
