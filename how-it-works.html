How It Works

Your journey from application to career placement — step by step

Our structured 13-step process takes you from where you are today to a high-paying career — with support at every step.

1

Inquire

Submit a quick interest form. Our team reaches out within 24–48 hours.

2

Overview Session

Attend a free orientation to learn about programs and funding options.

3

Membership

Complete your free enrollment and unlock access to all programs.

4

Assessment

Complete a skills and career assessment to identify your best pathway.

5

Interview

Meet with an advisor to confirm program fit and discuss your goals.

6

Resources

Get connected with wraparound support — transportation, childcare, and more.

7

Career Readiness

Complete workshops on professional communication and workplace expectations.

8

Career Direction

Finalize your career track and set measurable 90-day goals with your advisor.

9

Resume Building

Build a professional resume, optimize your LinkedIn, and prepare for interviews.

10

Training

Begin your funded certification program through Coursera, IBM, Google, or AWS.

11

Certification

Pass your industry exam. WorkforceAP covers exam fees and provides prep support.

12

Career Enhancement

Stack additional credentials and build your professional portfolio.

13

Job Placement

Get direct referrals, job fair access, and 100% job search support until placed.

🌟

Better Life & Future

You are now in a high-paying career with skills, credentials, and network to grow.
