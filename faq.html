Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WorkforceAP

Is this program really free?

Yes — 100% free to qualifying members. WorkforceAP is funded through WIOA, DOL grants, and employer partnerships. There are no hidden fees, no tuition, and no repayment required.

Who is eligible to apply?

WorkforceAP serves adults 18+ who are unemployed, underemployed, or seeking career advancement. We prioritize veterans, individuals with barriers to employment, and residents of Central Texas — though we serve learners statewide.

How long are the programs?

Most certification programs can be completed in 8–12 weeks depending on your pace. Some advanced tracks (like cloud or data science) may take 3–6 months.

Do I need prior experience or a degree?

No prior experience or degree required for most programs. We start from your current skill level and build from there. Basic computer literacy is helpful but not required.

What certifications will I earn?

Depending on your track, you may earn: Google Career Certificates, IBM Professional Certificates, AWS Cloud Practitioner, CompTIA A+/Network+/Security+, PMP/CAPM, and more — all industry-recognized credentials.

Will WorkforceAP help me find a job?

Yes. We provide 100% job search support including resume building, LinkedIn optimization, interview coaching, and direct employer referrals through our partner network.

Is training in-person or online?

Most training is delivered online through Coursera, IBM SkillsBuild, and Google Career Certificates — giving you flexibility to learn on your schedule. Some in-person workshops and employer connections are available in Austin.

How do I apply?

Click the Apply Now button, fill out the short application (under 10 minutes), and our team will contact you within 24–48 hours to schedule your orientation.

What if I have a criminal background?

WorkforceAP is committed to second-chance employment. We work with many employers who are open to candidates with backgrounds. Talk to an advisor about your specific situation.

Is childcare or transportation assistance available?

WorkforceAP can connect you with local resources for childcare, transportation assistance, and other wraparound services through our partner network.
