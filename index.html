Our Mission

Breaking Systemic Barriers

The Workforce Advancement Project exists to break down systemic barriers by providing no-cost funded digital literacy, artificial intelligence, occupational, and professional certification training to underserved individuals, adult learners, and veteran populations.

25+ Years of Impact
Our Vision & Mission

Opportunity for Everyone Who Wants to Grow

We empower individuals by delivering wrap-around services and resources — including assessment, workforce readiness, resume enhancement, occupational training, stackable career pathways, industry-recognized certificates, and job placement assistance.

Our vision is a world where every individual and family — regardless of background, income, or circumstance — has access to the skills and support necessary to thrive in the workforce today and lead the workforce of tomorrow.
