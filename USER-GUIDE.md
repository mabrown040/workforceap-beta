## For Students: How to Apply

### Step 1: Find Your Program (2 minutes)
1. Go to [workforceap.org](https://www.workforceap.org)
2. Click **"Find Your Path"** or **"Programs"**
3. Browse 19 programs or take the 2-minute quiz
4. The quiz asks about your interests, experience, and goals
5. Get matched to recommended programs

### Step 2: Submit Your Application (5 minutes)
1. Click **"Apply Now"** on any program page
2. Fill out the online form:
   - Contact information
   - Education and work history
   - Program interest
   - Referral source (how you heard about us)
3. Submit application

### Step 3: What Happens Next
| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Within 48 hours** | Counselor calls or emails you |
| **Within 1 week** | Schedule 30-minute interview |
| **Within 2 weeks** | Receive acceptance decision |
| **If accepted** | Get member portal login, start assessment |

### Step 4: Member Onboarding
Once accepted:
1. **Complete skills assessment** (30 minutes)
2. **Meet your assigned counselor**
3. **Get program orientation**
4. **Receive loaner laptop** (if needed)
5. **Start training**

## For Parents & Family

### What is WorkforceAP?
Workforce Advancement Project provides **free career training and industry certifications** for Austin-area residents looking to enter or advance in high-demand fields like technology, healthcare, and skilled trades.

### What Does "Free" Actually Mean?
- **No tuition costs** for accepted members
- **No loans** to pay back
- **No income sharing agreement** (ISA)
- **Loaner laptop provided** if you don't have one
- **All certification exam fees covered**

### Who Qualifies?
- Adults 18+ in the Austin metro area
- Income-eligible individuals (typically under 200% federal poverty level)
- Career changers welcome — no prior experience required
- Veterans and underserved communities prioritized

### What Programs Are Available?
19 programs across 7 categories:
- **IT & Cybersecurity:** CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, Google Cybersecurity
- **Cloud & Data:** AWS Cloud, Google Data Analytics, IBM Data Science
- **AI & Software:** IBM AI Developer, Software Developer
- **Business:** Project Management, Digital Marketing, UX Design
- **Healthcare:** Health Information Technology
- **Manufacturing:** Production Technology, Logistics, Construction Readiness
- **Digital Literacy:** Basic computer skills for beginners

### How Long Does It Take?
Most programs: **3-5 months** of part-time study (10 hours/week)

### What Certifications Will My Family Member Earn?
Industry-recognized certifications from:
- Google
- IBM
- Microsoft
- Amazon (AWS)
- CompTIA

### What Happens After Graduation?
- Job placement assistance provided
- Resume and interview support
- Direct connections to employers
- Average starting salary: $42K-$85K+ depending on program
- Alumni network access

### Is There Job Placement Support?
**Yes.** We provide:
- Resume review and optimization
- Interview practice and coaching
- Direct employer introductions
- Job search support until placement

**Note:** We don't "guarantee" jobs (no program can), but we support your search until you're hired.

### Safety & Support
- All counselors are background-checked
- Mental health resources available
- Childcare referrals provided if needed
- Transportation assistance available

## For Partners (Referral Organizations)

### What is a Partner?
Organizations that refer candidates to WorkforceAP, including:
- Workforce boards (Workforce Solutions, TWC)
- Community organizations
- Nonprofits
- Government agencies
- Faith-based organizations

### Partner Benefits
1. **Dedicated Portal** — Track your referred members in real-time
2. **Milestone Notifications** — Get email updates when your referrals:
   - Enroll in programs
   - Complete courses
   - Earn certifications
   - Get job placements
3. **Analytics Dashboard** — View aggregate stats on your referrals
4. **Direct Communication** — Priority contact channel with WorkforceAP staff

### How Referrals Work

#### Option 1: Send Them to Apply
1. Give candidates our website: workforceap.org
2. Have them select your organization in the "How did you hear about us?" field
3. They complete application normally
4. You receive confirmation email

#### Option 2: Admin Assignment
If a candidate applies without selecting your organization:
1. WorkforceAP admin can manually assign them to your partner record
2. You'll still receive milestone notifications

### Using the Partner Portal

#### Logging In
1. Go to workforceap.org/login
2. Use your partner credentials (provided by WorkforceAP staff)
3. You'll see "Partner View" indicator in the header

#### Dashboard Sections

**Stats Overview**
- Total members referred
- Active in training
- Completed programs
- Placed in jobs

**Members Table**
- Name, program, progress %
- Last active date
- Status (active, completed, placed)
- Click any member for detail view

**Recent Activity**
- Last 10 milestone events
- Enrollment notifications
- Completion updates
- Placement alerts

#### Member Detail View
What you can see:
- Program enrolled
- Progress percentage
- Courses completed
- Milestones achieved
- Placement status

What you **cannot** see (privacy protected):
- Contact information
- Assessment scores
- Personal details
- Benefit requests

### Getting Partner Portal Access

1. Contact Michael Brown: michael.brown@techvera.com

### Notification Preferences
You can customize which notifications you receive:
- New enrollments

- Job placements

Contact WorkforceAP staff to adjust your preferences.

## For Staff: Admin Guide

### Admin Login
1. Go to workforceap.org/login
2. Sign in with admin credentials
3. Click "Admin View" toggle in header

### Admin Sections

#### Members
- View all member accounts
- Add new members manually
- Edit member details
- Assign members to partners
- Search by name, email, or program

#### Partners
- View all partner organizations
- Add new partners
- Invite partner users
- View partner analytics
- Manage partner notifications

#### Programs
- View program enrollments
- Edit program details
- Manage curriculum

#### Blog
- Create and edit posts
- Manage categories
- Publish/unpublish

#### Analytics
- Cohort-level statistics
- Weekly recap data
- AI tools usage
- Certification tracking

### Adding a Member with Partner Assignment
1. Go to Admin → Members → Add Member
2. Fill out member information
3. In "Partner Referral" step, select partner from dropdown
4. Complete wizard
5. Member is created with PartnerReferral record

### Changing a Member's Partner
1. Go to Admin → Members
2. Find member, click to edit
3. Scroll to "Partner Assignment" section
4. Select new partner (or remove)
5. Save changes

### Partner Invite Flow
1. Go to Admin → Partners → [Partner Name]
2. Click "Invite Partner User"
3. Enter partner contact email
4. System sends invite with login credentials
5. Partner can log in and access portal

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Application & Eligibility

**Q: Is this really free? What's the catch?**  
A: Yes, completely free for accepted members. No tuition, no loans, no hidden fees. Funded through grants, employer partnerships, and workforce development contracts.

**Q: Who qualifies for WorkforceAP?**  
A: Adults 18+ in the Austin metro area who are income-eligible (typically earning under 200% of federal poverty level). Career changers welcome — no experience needed.

**Q: What if I don't have a computer?**  
A: We provide loaner laptops to all members who need them. You keep the laptop upon program completion.

**Q: Can I work while in the program?**  
A: Yes. Programs are designed for working adults — typically 10 hours/week of study time.

**Q: Do I need a high school diploma or GED?**  
A: Not required for most programs. We focus on your ability to learn and commitment to completing.

### Programs & Training

**Q: What certifications will I earn?**  
A: Depends on your program. Examples: Google IT Support, CompTIA A+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, IBM Data Science. Each program page lists specific certifications.

**Q: How long does each program take?**  
A: Most take 3-5 months of part-time study (10 hours/week). Digital Literacy is 6-7 weeks full-time.

**Q: Are classes online or in-person?**  
A: Most are online with some in-person support options. Some programs have hybrid components.

**Q: What if I fail a certification exam?**  
A: We cover one retake for most exams. Your counselor will work with you on additional prep.

### Job Placement

**Q: Do you guarantee job placement?**  
A: No legitimate program can guarantee jobs. We provide extensive job search support, employer connections, and stay with you until you're hired — but the actual hiring decision is up to employers.

**Q: What starting salaries do graduates earn?**  
A: Varies by program and experience:
- Digital Literacy: $38K-$52K
- IT Support: $55K-$72K
- Cybersecurity: $72K-$108K
- Data/Cloud: $88K-$145K

These are Austin-area medians; your results may vary.

**Q: What companies hire your graduates?**  
A: We work with employers across Austin including tech companies, healthcare systems, manufacturers, and government agencies. Specific employer list available upon request.

### Support & Services

**Q: What kind of support do you provide?**  
A: Every member gets:
- Assigned career counselor
- Resume and interview help
- Job search strategy
- Technical support
- Mental health referrals
- Childcare and transportation referrals (when available)

**Q: Can I switch programs once I start?**  
A: Yes, within the first 2 weeks. After that, we evaluate on a case-by-case basis.

**Q: What if I need to pause my training?**  
A: Life happens. Contact your counselor to discuss a leave of absence. Most members can resume within 6 months.

### For Partners

**Q: How do I become a referring partner?**  
A: Contact Michael Brown at michael.brown@techvera.com with your organization details.

**Q: Do you pay referral fees?**  
A: No. We don't pay for referrals. Partners refer because they trust our program and want the best outcomes for their clients.

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**  
A: Partners get portal access with real-time tracking of referred members' progress.

**Q: What information do partners receive?**  
A: Milestone notifications (enrollment, completion, placement) and aggregate statistics. Individual member details are privacy-protected.
