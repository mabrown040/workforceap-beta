# AI Prompt Pack for Resume Rewriting

Use these prompts with ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI tools to improve your resume.

## General Resume Improvement

> "Review my resume for [target role]. Suggest stronger action verbs and quantify achievements where possible. Keep it to one page."

## Tailoring to a Job

> "I'm applying for [job title] at [company]. Here's the job description: [paste]. Tailor my resume bullets to match their requirements. Keep my actual experience accurate."

## Bullet Strengthening

> "Rewrite these resume bullets to be more impactful. Use the formula: [Action verb] + [what you did] + [measurable result]. [paste bullets]"

## Summary Statement

> "Write a 2–3 sentence professional summary for someone with [X years] experience in [field], targeting [role]. Highlight [key skill 1] and [key skill 2]."

## Important Reminder

Always fact-check AI output. Never claim skills or achievements you don't have.
