# Career Pivot Roadmap (30/60/90)

A structured approach to transitioning into a new field.

## First 30 Days: Research & Foundation

- **Research** target roles and required skills
- **Identify** 2–3 transferable skills from current experience
- **Enroll** in one course or certification
- **Update** resume and LinkedIn for new direction
- **Connect** with 10 people in target industry

## Days 31–60: Build & Apply

- **Complete** or progress in learning path
- **Apply** to 15–20 roles (even if "stretch")
- **Informational interviews** with 3–5 people in target field
- **Refine** resume based on application feedback
- **Join** relevant professional groups or communities

## Days 61–90: Execute & Iterate

- **Apply** to 20+ roles
- **Interview** — use each as practice
- **Follow up** on all applications
- **Adjust** strategy based on response rates
- **Consider** contract or project work to build experience

## Key Mindset

Pivots take time. Focus on progress, not perfection. Each application and conversation builds momentum.
