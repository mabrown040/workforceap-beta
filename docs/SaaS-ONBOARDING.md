# WorkforceAP SaaS Onboarding Notes

Quick-start guide for admins and partners.

## For Administrators

### First-time setup
1. **Sign in** at `/login` with your admin account.
2. **Admin portal** at `/admin` — dashboard, members, partners, jobs, pipeline.
3. **Job approvals** — Employers post jobs; you approve at Admin → Jobs.
4. **AI matching** — After approving a job, use "Suggest matches" to notify employer of candidate fits.
5. **Placements** — Record student placements under Admin → Pipeline or member profile.

### Key paths
- `/admin` — Main admin dashboard
- `/admin/jobs` — Pending and live job postings
- `/admin/members` — Member directory
- `/admin/partners` — Partner orgs and referrals

## For Partners

### Partner portal
1. **Sign in** with your partner-invited account.
2. **Partner dashboard** at `/partner` — see referred members, progress, placements.
3. **Member detail** — Click a member to view program progress, readiness, notes.

### Referrals
- Members are linked to your org via referral source or admin assignment.
- Progress updates (courses, assessments) appear on your dashboard.

## For Employers

### Employer portal
1. **Sign in** with your employer account (created by admin).
2. **Dashboard** at `/employer` — post jobs, view applications.
3. **Post job** — Create at `/employer/jobs/new` or import from LinkedIn.
4. **Applications** — View student applications per job at `/employer/applications`.

### Workflow
- Post job → Admin reviews → Approved jobs go live → Students apply → You manage applications.

## Super Admin (Staff)

- **View switcher** — If you have `super_admin` role, a "View:" dropdown appears in portal headers.
- Switch between Admin, Partner, Student, and Employer portals without signing out.
- Cookie/session persists across portal hops.
