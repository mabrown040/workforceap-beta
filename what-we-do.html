What we do

Breaking down systemic barriers through education, technology, and opportunity.

WorkforceAP exists to make career advancement feel reachable, supported, and grounded in real labor-market outcomes.

The Work

Funded career pathways with human support at every stage.

We provide no-cost funded digital literacy, artificial intelligence, occupational, and professional certification training to underserved individuals, adult learners, and veterans who are ready to build stronger economic futures.

  • Assessment and readiness coaching before training begins
  • Industry-recognized certifications that align with employer demand
  • Resume, interview, and job placement support after completion
🎯

Mission

The Workforce Advancement Project exists to expand access to funded training, reduce systemic barriers, and equip people with the credentials, confidence, and support needed to enter sustainable careers.

🌍

Vision

We envision a future where every person and family — regardless of income, background, or circumstance — can access the skills, opportunity, and support needed to thrive in the workforce of today and tomorrow.
