# STAR Story Worksheet

Use this framework to structure your interview answers.

## STAR = Situation, Task, Action, Result

### Situation
*Set the scene. Where were you? What was the context?*
- Company/role
- Timeline
- Relevant background

### Task
*What was your responsibility? What needed to be done?*
- Your specific goal
- Constraints or challenges

### Action
*What did YOU do? Use "I" not "we."*
- Steps you took
- Decisions you made
- Skills you used

### Result
*What happened? Quantify when possible.*
- Outcome
- Metrics (%, $, time saved)
- What you learned
