# Using AI Ethically in Your Job Search

AI can help—but use it responsibly.

## Do Use AI For

- **Brainstorming** bullet points and phrasing
- **Improving** grammar and clarity
- **Tailoring** language to job descriptions
- **Practicing** interview questions
- **Researching** companies and roles

## Don't Use AI For

- **Inventing** experience, skills, or achievements
- **Writing** entire applications without review
- **Submitting** content you don't understand
- **Faking** assessments or take-home assignments

## Best Practices

1. **Always fact-check** — AI can hallucinate. Verify dates, titles, and claims.
2. **Make it yours** — Edit AI output to sound like you. Add your voice.
3. **Be honest** — If asked in an interview, disclose AI use for drafting. Lying is worse.
4. **Understand what you submit** — You must be able to discuss everything on your resume.

## Bottom Line

AI is a tool. You are responsible for the accuracy and authenticity of everything you submit.
