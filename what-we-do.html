What We Do

Breaking down systemic barriers through education, technology, and opportunity.

🎯

Mission

The Workforce Advancement Project exists to break down systemic barriers by providing no-cost funded digital literacy, artificial intelligence, occupational, and professional certification training to underserved individuals, adult learners, and veteran populations.

🌍

Vision

We are working to create a world where every individual and family — regardless of background, income, opportunity, or circumstance — has access to the skills, opportunity, and support necessary to thrive in the workforce today and lead the workforce of tomorrow.
