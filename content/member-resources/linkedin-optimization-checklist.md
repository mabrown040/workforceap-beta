# LinkedIn Profile Optimization Checklist

Make your profile visible to recruiters and hiring managers.

## Headline
- [ ] Include your target role or industry
- [ ] Add 2–3 key skills or value props
- [ ] Keep under 120 characters

## Photo
- [ ] Professional headshot, clear face
- [ ] Neutral background
- [ ] Recent (within 2 years)

## About / Summary
- [ ] First 2 lines are compelling (they show in search)
- [ ] Include keywords from your target jobs
- [ ] Mention your goals and what you offer

## Experience
- [ ] Each role has 3–5 bullet points
- [ ] Use action verbs and metrics
- [ ] Align with target role keywords

## Skills
- [ ] Add at least 15 relevant skills
- [ ] Reorder to highlight top 3
- [ ] Get endorsements from connections

## Other
- [ ] Custom URL (linkedin.com/in/yourname)
- [ ] Open to Work (if job seeking)
- [ ] Connect with recruiters in your industry
