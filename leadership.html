Meet the team

Experienced leaders committed to access and advancement.

Executive Director, CEO

Michael Brown, PMP

Business executive and workforce leader with decades of experience across education, project management, and organizational growth.

Read bio

Michael Brown, PMP, is a highly accomplished business executive with a distinguished career spanning business development, project management, and education. As former owner of Consulting Solutions.Net and a key leader at State of Texas Career Schools, Goodwill Central Texas, and Austin Urban League, he has consistently driven excellence across public and private sectors. He is also an active member of 100 Black Men of Austin and Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity.

Chief Operating Officer

Adriane Brown

Operations leader focused on growth, compliance, execution, and building systems that scale mission-driven work.

Read bio

Adriane Brown is a strategic business and operations leader with more than 25 years of experience driving organizational growth and operational excellence. A Texas A&M graduate, she has led enterprise operations to national reach and brings deep expertise in business development, compliance, and performance optimization. She completed Microsoft Project Management Certification in 2025 and serves as Women’s Ministry Leader at Celebration Church.

Board Member

Lakecia Gunter

Senior technology executive bringing national perspective on innovation, engineering leadership, and partnerships.

Read bio

Lakecia Gunter is a 25-year tech industry veteran serving as Chief Technology Officer, Global Partner Solutions at Microsoft. Honored as a finalist in SUCCESS Magazine’s 2023 Women of Influence, she is a nationally recognized engineer, speaker, and advocate for women in technology. She holds an MS in Electrical Engineering from Georgia Tech and currently serves on the Board of Directors at IDEX Corporation.

Board Member

Brandon Frye

Entrepreneur and business operator with experience building, scaling, and advising high-growth organizations.

Read bio

Brandon Frye is a seasoned entrepreneur and business operator with extensive experience founding, investing in, and leading high-growth companies. He co-founded Interstate Connections, recognized by Austin Business Journal as the fastest-growing private company, and currently serves as CFO of The Business Bible. He is active across community leadership and board service.

Board Member

Colonel Derrick Fishback (Ret.)

Transformational leader with military and enterprise experience spanning workforce leadership and cloud transformation.

Read bio

Colonel Derrick Fishback (Retired) is a transformational leader with nearly three decades of U.S. Army service complemented by leadership roles at Fortune 500 companies. He has commanded more than 1,800 personnel and led cloud transformation initiatives at Amazon Web Services, IBM, and Dell. Derrick holds two master’s degrees and serves as Board President of the Jazz Society of Pensacola.
