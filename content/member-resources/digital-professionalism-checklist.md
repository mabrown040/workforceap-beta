# Digital Professionalism & Workplace Readiness Checklist

Prepare for a professional environment.

## Email & Communication
- [ ] Professional email address (firstname.lastname, avoid nicknames)
- [ ] Clear subject lines
- [ ] Respond within 24–48 hours
- [ ] Proofread before sending
- [ ] Use appropriate tone (formal for first contact)

## Online Presence
- [ ] LinkedIn profile complete and professional
- [ ] Social media set to private or cleaned up
- [ ] No inappropriate posts visible to employers
- [ ] Consistent name/photos across platforms

## Virtual Meetings
- [ ] Test camera and mic beforehand
- [ ] Neutral, professional background
- [ ] Join on time
- [ ] Mute when not speaking
- [ ] Dress appropriately (at least from waist up)

## Workplace Basics
- [ ] Reliable internet and device
- [ ] Quiet space for calls
- [ ] Calendar for scheduling
- [ ] Follow-up on commitments
