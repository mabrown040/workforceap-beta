Michael Brown, PMP

Executive Director, CEO

Michael Brown, PMP, is a highly accomplished business executive with a distinguished career spanning several decades in business development, project management, and education. As former owner of Consulting Solutions.Net and key leader at State of Texas Career Schools, Goodwill Central Texas, and Austin Urban League, he has consistently driven organizational excellence across public and private sectors.

A devoted community leader, Michael is an active member of 100 Black Men of Austin and Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity. His deep roots in Austin's workforce ecosystem and his PMP certification make him uniquely positioned to lead WorkforceAP's mission of expanding economic opportunity for all.

Adriane Brown

COO

Adriane Brown serves as Chief Operating Officer of the Workforce Advancement Project, overseeing day-to-day operations, program delivery, and organizational strategy. She brings extensive experience in workforce development and organizational management to the team.

Lakecia Gunter

Board Member

Lakecia Gunter serves on the WorkforceAP Board of Directors, bringing leadership expertise and community perspective to guide the organization's strategic direction and mission of workforce advancement across Central Texas.

Brandon Frye

Board Member

Brandon Frye serves on the WorkforceAP Board of Directors, contributing expertise in career development and community engagement to support the organization's workforce training programs and strategic initiatives.

Colonel Derrick Fishback (Retired)

Board Member

Colonel Derrick Fishback (Retired) brings decades of military leadership and operational experience to the WorkforceAP Board. His background in veteran transition and workforce readiness programs makes him a vital advocate for veteran members of our community.
